How DriveScore Works

A transparent, data-driven system — just like CIBIL for driving

1

Data Collection

Traffic cameras, e-challan system, ITMS, FASTag, and police reports feed violations into DriveScore in real-time.

2

Score Calculation

AI engine processes violations by severity, frequency, and recency. Positive behaviors boost your score over time.

3

Score Assigned

Every driver gets a score from 300-900, categorized into tiers from Red Zone to Platinum Driver.

4

Real-World Impact

Insurance companies, RTOs, employers, and lenders use your score for pricing, licensing, and decisions.
